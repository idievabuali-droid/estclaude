# Real Estate Platform — Strong PRD v3

## 1. Product summary

### Working product definition
A responsive bilingual website that helps serious buyers of new-build apartments in Dushanbe and Vahdat **find the right apartment faster**, **compare clearly**, **trust what they see**, and **contact sellers with confidence**.

### Core idea
This is not another classifieds site. It is a decision platform designed to make new-build apartment buying feel clear, trustworthy, and manageable in a market where today it feels confusing, risky, and opaque.

The platform wins by doing one job better than anyone else in Tajikistan:

**help serious buyers confidently choose the right new-build apartment faster than they can on Somon or anywhere else.**

### Product category
- New-build apartment decision platform
- Trust-first real estate platform for Tajikistan's primary market
- Qualified lead engine for developers

## 5. Problem

### 5.1 Buyer problems

Today, buyers of new-build apartments in Tajikistan face:
- listings scattered across classifieds with no project-first structure
- no reliable way to know whether a developer will actually deliver
- no price-per-m² transparency and no neighborhood benchmarks
- unclear finishing types, often hidden in free-text descriptions
- unclear installment terms and payment plans
- no way to compare two or three projects or units side by side
- fear of paying and losing money to developers who don't finish
- difficulty involving family in the decision
- language barriers — most platforms are Russian-only
- diaspora buyers in Russia cannot evaluate projects remotely with any confidence

### 5.2 Developer problems

Developers face:
- weak lead quality on classifieds
- repeated conversations with low-intent buyers
- difficulty presenting their project with structured, trust-worthy content
- no way to differentiate themselves from less reliable developers
- poor visibility into what buyers actually want
- no credible channel for serious buyers

### 5.3 Seller problems (owners and intermediaries)

Owners and intermediaries face:
- classifieds that treat all listings equally regardless of honesty
- no way to earn visible trust for quality listings
- unreliable traffic and inconsistent lead quality
- no visual differentiation between real listings and scams

What's missing is **decision support before contact** and **trust that reduces anxiety before spending life savings**.

So the thesis is:

**If we help buyers narrow faster, compare more clearly, and trust the information more, they will choose us during the serious decision stage — and developers, owners, and intermediaries will follow because the buyers on our platform are worth more.**

## 7. Product principles

### 7.1 Decision first
Every important screen must help the buyer move closer to a decision.

### 7.2 Don't fight natural user behavior
Let buyers browse fast. Never force them through a long wizard before they can see value.

### 7.3 Trust is a product feature
Trust comes from structured evidence, not marketing. Verification badges, dated photos, and honest labels are product features, not operational afterthoughts.

### 7.4 Honesty is a moat
Every listing states honestly who the seller is. Every price is shown with fair-value context. Every finishing type is labeled. Platforms that mislead their users are copying features. Platforms that tell the truth build loyalty.

### 7.5 Explain, don't just display
Every important piece of information should answer "what does this mean for me?" A price shown with a "12% below district average" badge is more useful than the same price alone.

### 7.6 Simplicity over clutter
A smaller set of strong features is always better than a large, messy feature set. Fewer filters done well beat eighty filters that return zero results.

### 7.7 Halal by design
The platform itself contains no haram mechanics. No interest rates. No deceptive urgency. No gambling loops. No misleading scarcity. This is a product rule, not a religious label applied to individual listings.

### 7.8 Narrow first, expand later
We win Dushanbe and Vahdat new-builds completely before expanding to other cities, rentals, or resales.

### 7.9 Retention starts with real value
Return visits are earned through saved shortlists, honest updates, real comparison tools, and a product families will discuss together — not through notifications and growth hacks.

### 7.10 Mobile-first, always
Most buyers will use the platform on their phone. Every design decision must work beautifully on a phone first.

## 11. The three-source inventory model

Every listing on the platform belongs to exactly one of three source types, declared honestly by the seller at posting time.

### 11.1 🏗 От застройщика (From developer)

Listings from the developer directly, whether via the developer's own sales office or through a sales partner authorized to represent them. Typically new units from active inventory, often unfinished or pre-finish, often with installment plans.

### 11.2 👤 Собственник (Owner)

Listings where the seller is selling an apartment that belongs to them or their family. The legal document may be registered in any family member's name — what matters is the apartment belongs to the seller's family and the seller has real authority to represent the sale. When the final transaction happens, the family member whose name is on the document will be present.

This category includes flippers selling renovated units registered in a parent's name, family members selling for relatives, and owners selling their own units.

### 11.3 🤝 Посредник (Intermediary)

Listings where the seller is selling an apartment that belongs to someone outside their family. They do not own the apartment — they are marketing it on behalf of the real owner, typically for commission.

### 11.4 Why three sources, not two and not four

The new-build market in Tajikistan genuinely includes all three types of sellers. Excluding any of them excludes real inventory buyers want to see. But merging them together hides meaningful information — a buyer's risk profile is different when dealing with an owner versus an intermediary.

Three sources, labeled honestly, is the cleanest reflection of how the market actually works.

### 11.5 The building is the canonical entity

Every listing attaches to a building. The building is the shared anchor that lets all three source types coexist without confusion.

On a building page, listings are organized in two clear sections:
- **Новые квартиры от застройщика** — current developer inventory
- **Перепродажа** — owner and intermediary listings

The developer controls project-level information (name, description, handover date, amenities, official gallery). Resellers can only list inside buildings that already exist on the platform — they cannot invent or rewrite project data.

## 12. The three-tier verification ladder

Verification is optional and earned. It creates visible trust without blocking the act of posting.

### 12.1 Core principle

**Post first, verify later.** Posting a listing requires only a verified phone number. No documents. No signed papers. No proof of ownership.

This matches how successful regional platforms operate (Krisha.kz, Avito, Cian). Requiring documents upfront kills supply.

### 12.2 Tier 1 — ✓ Номер подтверждён (Phone confirmed)

Automatic on signup via SMS OTP. Every listing starts here. Gray checkmark badge.

### 12.3 Tier 2 — ✓ Профиль проверен (Profile verified)

Earned when the seller uploads a selfie with their national ID. Platform manually confirms the face matches the document. No property papers required. 2-minute flow. Blue checkmark badge. Higher ranking in search results.

### 12.4 Tier 3 — ✓ Объект проверен (Listing verified on-site)

Earned when a platform representative physically visits the apartment, meets the seller, takes geo-tagged photos, and confirms the listing is real. For intermediary listings, this tier additionally requires a verified phone call with the real owner confirming the seller's permission. No signed documents required. Valid 45 days for sale listings. Green checkmark badge with verification date. Top ranking in search results.

### 12.5 Developer verification

Accounts listing as 🏗 От застройщика pass a one-time developer check: the platform calls the developer's publicly known office to confirm the account is authorized. Once confirmed, all listings from that developer inherit verified-developer status.

### 12.6 How trust is shown to buyers

Every listing card shows the source chip and the highest trust badge the listing has earned. Listings rank in search results by trust tier first, then freshness, then relevance. Unverified listings are never deleted — they simply rank lower. Sellers see the ranking difference and self-motivate to verify.

### 12.7 Fraud prevention

Beyond the tier system, the platform prevents fraud through:
- duplicate photo detection via perceptual hashing
- building + floor + unit deduplication
- user reporting with three-strike suspension
- mystery-buyer spot checks on random listings
- behavioral pattern detection for suspicious posting activity

## 13. The core buyer journey

**Land → understand → browse → narrow → evaluate → save → compare → contact.**

The buyer can complete the full journey without registering. Registration is only required when the buyer wants to save items or contact a seller.

### Step 1 — Land and understand
Buyer lands on the homepage and understands within 10 seconds:
- this is for new-build apartments in Dushanbe and Vahdat
- every project is verified by the platform team
- they can browse by building or by individual apartment

### Step 2 — Browse
Buyer chooses one of two browsing modes:
- **Новостройки** (projects-first): browse buildings, then units within them
- **Квартиры** (apartments-first): browse individual apartments across all buildings

### Step 3 — Narrow
Buyer filters by price, rooms, size, finishing, district, delivery date, source, verification level, monthly installment amount.

### Step 4 — Evaluate
Buyer opens a building page or a listing page and sees structured, honest information — price-per-m², fairness badge, finishing label, installment terms, construction photos, trust badge.

### Step 5 — Save and compare
Buyer saves shortlist items, returns across sessions, compares 2–4 options side by side, optionally shares with family.

### Step 6 — Contact
Buyer taps WhatsApp, Call, or Request Visit — low-friction and familiar.

## 14. Two browsing modes

Different buyers shop differently. The platform supports both mental models cleanly.

### 14.1 Project-first mode (Новостройки)

Buyers browse buildings as primary objects. They see the building's status, delivery date, developer, trust status, and available inventory at a glance. Then they drill into units within a chosen building.

This mode is for buyers who care about the building first — location, developer reputation, delivery timing — and the unit second.

### 14.2 Apartment-first mode (Квартиры)

Buyers search directly for apartments across the whole platform, filtered by budget, rooms, size, finishing, and installment amount. Every result card is clearly labeled with its source chip (🏗 / 👤 / 🤝).

This mode is for buyers who know their constraints first — budget, rooms, monthly payment — and want to see every apartment that fits, regardless of which building it's in.

### 14.3 Why both, not one

Some buyers start with "I want to live in this building" and narrow down to a unit. Others start with "I can afford 8,000 TJS/month" and see what's available. Forcing one mental model excludes the other. Having two top-level tabs with distinct URLs, distinct results pages, and clearly different visual treatments makes both modes immediately understandable.

## 19. Halal-by-design rules

The platform does not label individual listings as halal or haram. The platform itself is free of haram mechanics.

### 19.1 Never in the product

- No interest-based mortgage calculator
- No "X% годовых" displays
- No fake urgency mechanics ("3 people viewing now", countdown timers)
- No deceptive scarcity
- No gambling-style rewards
- No alcohol, bar, or casino imagery
- No misleading scarcity claims
- No dark-pattern consent flows

### 19.2 Present in the product

- Installment plans as the hero affordability tool
- Honest inventory counts with last-updated timestamps
- Amenity distances to mosque, Islamic school, bazaar, transport
- Optional "Prefer female agent" toggle in contact flow
- Optional "Contact my family member instead" option
- Plain, unambiguous copy in both Russian and Tajik

### 19.3 Finance partnerships

If financial product integrations are added in later phases, only Shariah-compliant partners (Alif Bank, Tawhidbank) are acceptable. Conventional interest-bearing products are never displayed on listings.

## 25. Risks

### Risk 1 — Becoming another classifieds site
If the product feels like Somon with a nicer UI, switching motivation is weak. The defense is the verification system, the construction photos, the honest labels, and the bilingual Tajik experience.

### Risk 2 — Supply collapse from friction
If posting requires documents, supply dies. The defense is post-first-verify-later with earned trust tiers.

### Risk 3 — Trust collapse from unverified listings
If unverified listings mix confusingly with verified ones, trust dilutes. The defense is clear visual tiering, ranking separation, and honest badges on every listing.

### Risk 4 — Weak data quality
If project and unit data is incomplete or stale, the whole platform weakens. The defense is curated launch with only 15–30 complete buildings, grown carefully.

### Risk 5 — Scope expansion too early
If rentals, resales of old housing, or other cities are added before V1 wins Dushanbe and Vahdat, the wedge dilutes. The defense is disciplined V1 scope.

### Risk 6 — Copycat from Somon
If Somon copies visible features, their traffic advantage is dangerous. The defense is operational moats — construction photos, verified inventory, developer relationships, bilingual content — that require on-the-ground work Somon won't invest in.

The product is **a responsive bilingual decision platform for serious buyers of new-build apartments in Dushanbe and Vahdat, with honest labeled listings from developers, owners, and intermediaries, earned trust through a three-tier verification system, construction-progress photos taken by the platform team, transparent price-per-m² context, installment-based affordability as the hero financial tool, full Russian and Tajik language support, and a diaspora entry point for Tajik migrants in Russia.**

This is the strong version of the platform — narrow enough to launch cleanly, complete enough to actually help buyers decide, and defensible enough that Somon cannot copy it without rebuilding their entire trust posture from scratch.
